Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Summary: Uso Yokai Bot collects only the minimum data necessary to post content to your TikTok account. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for any purpose other than providing the Service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Uso Yokai Bot, we may collect the following information:

  • TikTok Account Information: Your TikTok username, user ID, and display name as provided by TikTok's API upon authorization
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok that allow us to post content on your behalf
  • Posting Permissions: The specific permissions you grant during TikTok OAuth authorization (e.g., video.publish)
  • Content Data: Videos and metadata you configure for automated posting
  • Usage Logs: Basic logs of posting activity (timestamps, success/failure status) for operational purposes

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your TikTok password or login credentials
  • Personal identification information beyond what TikTok provides via API
  • Financial or payment information
  • Data from your followers or other TikTok users
  • Browsing history or behavioral tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Content Posting: To authenticate with TikTok and post content to your account as configured
  • Service Operation: To maintain posting schedules and manage the bot's operation
  • Troubleshooting: To diagnose and resolve technical issues with the Service

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond the core functionality described above.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • TikTok: Data is transmitted to TikTok as required to post content through their API
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Service Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., cloud hosting) under strict data processing agreements

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and account information are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • Access tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Data transmission is secured via HTTPS/TLS
  • Access to stored credentials is restricted to authorized systems only

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service:

  • Active accounts: Data is retained while you are actively using the Service
  • Revocation: If you revoke authorization through TikTok's settings, we will delete your access tokens within 30 days
  • Deletion request: You may request complete deletion of your data by contacting us. We will process such requests within 30 days.
  • Usage logs: Operational logs are retained for up to 90 days, then permanently deleted

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and disconnect your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app settings
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

8. TikTok API Data Usage

Uso Yokai Bot uses TikTok's official API. By using this Service, you also agree to TikTok's Privacy Policy. Data obtained through TikTok's API is used only in accordance with TikTok's API Terms of Service. We do not store TikTok user data beyond what is required for the Service to function.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
